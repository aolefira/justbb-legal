# Privacy Policy for JustBB

**Effective date:** 2026-05-24
**Last updated:** 2026-05-24

This Privacy Policy describes how the JustBB iOS application ("JustBB", "the app", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. JustBB is developed and operated by Andrew Olefira ("the developer").

## Summary

JustBB is a personal strength-training log. **All your workout data — exercises, workouts, sessions, sets, weights, reps, ratings, folders — is stored only on your device.** It is never transmitted to us, to any server, or to any third party. We do not operate a backend, we do not have user accounts, and we do not sync your data to any cloud service we control.

If you have iCloud Drive enabled on your iPhone, the app optionally saves backup copies of your data to your own personal iCloud Drive container (see section 4). These backups remain inside your Apple iCloud account and are not accessible to the developer.

The only information the app sends off-device to a service the developer operates is **anonymous crash diagnostics** through Firebase Crashlytics, used solely to detect and fix bugs.

## Health & Fitness Disclaimer

JustBB is a personal training log, not medical advice. Consult a physician before starting any exercise program.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

JustBB does **not** collect, request, or transmit any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, age, gender, body measurements, or any other personally identifiable information.
- Your location (GPS, Wi-Fi, IP-based).
- Your contacts, photos, microphone input, or camera input.
- Your workout content (exercises, weights, reps, sessions, ratings, notes, folder names).
- Your usage patterns, screen views, taps, or session duration.
- Your device identifiers used for advertising (IDFA).
- Any information that could identify you personally.

JustBB does not have user accounts, registration, sign-in, or any cloud profile.

## 2. Information Collected Automatically (Crash Diagnostics)

JustBB uses Firebase Crashlytics (a service provided by Google) to receive automatic reports when the app crashes or encounters a critical error. These reports help the developer identify and fix bugs.

A crash report contains:

- **Stack trace** — the code path that led to the crash.
- **Device model** — e.g., "iPhone 15 Pro".
- **iOS version** — e.g., "17.4".
- **App version and build number** — e.g., "2026.05.03 (1)".
- **A randomly generated installation identifier** assigned by Firebase, which cannot be used to identify you personally and is not linked to any account or contact information.

Crash reports do **not** contain your workout data, your name, your email, your IP address, or any other content you create in the app.

Firebase Crashlytics' handling of this data is governed by Google's privacy practices: <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.

In Apple's App Privacy framework, this data is categorized as **"Diagnostics" → "Crash Data"**, **not linked to user identity**.

## 3. Local Storage on Your Device

All content you create in JustBB — workouts, exercises, sessions, folders, settings — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

- Remains on your device only.
- Is included in your iPhone's standard local backup (encrypted iCloud Backup or local iTunes/Finder backup) if you have iPhone backups enabled. Such backups are managed entirely by Apple under Apple's privacy terms, not by us. We have no access to backup contents.
- Is deleted permanently when you delete the JustBB app from your device, unless restored from a backup.

## 4. iCloud Drive Backups (Optional)

If you have iCloud Drive enabled on your iPhone and have not disabled iCloud Drive access for JustBB, the app automatically saves rotating backup copies of your local workout database to your personal iCloud Drive container after each completed session. Up to 10 most recent backups are retained; older ones are deleted automatically.

These backup files remain entirely within your own Apple iCloud account. The developer has no access to them and cannot view, modify, or recover them. They are stored and protected under Apple's iCloud privacy terms: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

You can disable iCloud Drive backups for JustBB at any time in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud → JustBB. You can also delete individual backups from within the app (Settings → Backups → iCloud backups) or from the Files app on your iPhone.

## 5. In-App Purchases (Tip Jar)

JustBB offers an optional "Tip Jar" — non-subscription in-app purchases that let you support the developer. All transactions are processed entirely by Apple through Apple's StoreKit framework. The developer does not receive, see, or store your payment method, billing address, name, or any other financial or personal information related to the purchase.

Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's privacy policy: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## 6. Third-Party Services

JustBB integrates the following third-party service only:

- **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google LLC) — for anonymous crash diagnostics. See section 2.

JustBB does **not** integrate:

- Analytics services that track user behavior.
- Advertising networks.
- Social media SDKs.
- Login or sign-in providers.
- Any data broker or marketing tool.

## 7. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share any data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purpose. The only data leaving your device to a service the developer operates is the anonymous crash diagnostics described in section 2, sent only to Firebase Crashlytics for the purpose of diagnosing app failures.

## 8. Children's Privacy

JustBB is rated 4+ in the App Store and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from any user, including children under 13. Since the app does not request or collect personal information from any user, the requirements of the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations are addressed by the fact that no such information is gathered.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

Because JustBB stores your content only on your device and we do not maintain user accounts or profiles, you have full control:

- **Access:** Open the app to see all your data — there is no separate copy held elsewhere by the developer.
- **Modification:** Edit or delete any exercise, workout, or session directly in the app.
- **Deletion:** Uninstall the app to remove all local data permanently (subject to any backups you have configured through Apple — see sections 3 and 4).
- **Crash diagnostics opt-out:** You can disable analytics and crash reporting collection for all apps system-wide through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements → Share iPhone Analytics. Disabling this setting prevents Firebase Crashlytics from receiving reports from your device.

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have certain rights regarding personal data. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, we hold no personal data about you that could be accessed, corrected, exported, or deleted upon request. If you believe we hold information about you and wish to exercise these rights, please contact us (see section 12).

## 10. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data about you, because we do not collect any.

Crash diagnostics held by Firebase Crashlytics are retained according to Google's retention policies (typically 90 days for full reports, longer for aggregated statistics). See <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated through an app update. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
